            \begin{solution}

                \begin{align*}
                    T(s) &= (\lambda,\ \lambda^2, \lambda^2,\ \lambda^3, \lambda^3, \lambda^3, \lambda^3, \ \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, \lambda^4, ...  )\\

                \end{align*}
                So any real number is an eigenvalue of $T$.
